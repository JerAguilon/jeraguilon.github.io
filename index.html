Summer 2017

Amazon Software Engineering Intern (Personalization)

Personalization deals with recommending new products and services to customers. Naturally, one of the biggest challenges with cataloging Amazon's huge array of products is latency. In order to incorporate more complex (and by extension more relevant) algorithms, there is a tradeoff in computational cost. In order to include this more sophisticated model in widgets with low latency requirements, I created a NoSQL solution with preprocessed recommendation scores for over 100 million listings.

School Initiatives

UROC Undergraduate Researcher
Full stack developer, Winter 2016

Asthma is a chronic condition that afflicts at a higher rate for those of low socioeconomic status. One of the most effective ways for parents to manage their childrens' asthma is through an Asthma Action Plan. Research has shown that icon-based plans have improved the health care management of those with lower reading comprehension. My role under Dr. Rosa Arriaga was to implement the backend of the IBAAP with the front end prototype.

The Clean Water Project (Java) [link]

A group project for CS2340, Georgia Tech's Object Oriented Design class. 783 million people live without steady access to water. This project aims to provide a means of tracking where clean water is available. We follow a crowd sourced approach, allowing users to enter where clean water has been found. Moreover, managers can track the cleanliness of water in particular locations over time.
